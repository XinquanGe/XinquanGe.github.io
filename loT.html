Internet of Things

The purpose of the Internet of things is to connect anything with the Internet for information exchange and communication. Through
sensors, the Internet of things can judge the current state and make appropriate feedback.

For example, Internet of things technology is used in household appliances.
You can use mobile devices to control them remotely. At the same time, the
safety of these appliances is also greatly improved. In the state of networking,
they are not prone to fire, and they will give early warning in case of failure.
