Big data and Blockchain

Information society is seen as a successor to industrial society. In the development of human society, from paper letters to telegraph and
telephone. The digitization of information has brought about a qualitative change in the speed of information dissemination. In today's
information society, big data and Blockchain brings innovation to information again.

Big data usually includes data sets with sizes beyond the ability of commonly used software tools to capture, curate, manage, and process
data within a tolerable elapsed time. Generally speaking, when a data set reaches the size of TB and Pb, it can be called big data. At the
same time, it should have extremely fast data processing speed and flexible data structure to deal with massive data.

The application of big data makes the transmission and collection of information faster and can save more information. This benefits from
the development of digitization and computers. But at the same time, big data also brings problems and doubts. Big data is relatively fuzzy
and contains some false information. The opacity of information content also makes many people question the information security of big
data. At this time, blockchain gives us an answer.

Blockchain originated from bitcoin. Blockchain is a shared account book and database, which has the characteristics of non-tampering, tracing,
openness and transparency. The source and destination of information are completely transparent. If blockchain and big data are used together,
users' data can be controlled by themselves.

Big data and blockchain can be used in many places, such as network security, artificial intelligence. One of the most noteworthy is the medical
record and healthcare. All medical records are clearly visible, and all changes and interpretations to the records are maintained in a transparent
manner. At the same time, personalize healthcare and create best practices for working with consumers or patients. Everyone can benefit from it.
