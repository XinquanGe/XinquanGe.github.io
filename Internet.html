Internet

The most basic part of the digital society from the end of 20 century.
Since the end of the 20th century, the Internet has already met the public for
nearly 40 years. Internet technology has been relatively perfect and mature.

The Internet was created through TCP / IP protocol. They are just like the
postmen on the Internet, and our addresses on the Internet. With this
technology as a guarantee, more Internet applications have been developed.
These applications, which are closest to our life, are the world wide web (WWW).

Nowadays, with a computer or mobile phone connected to the Internet, users
can get all the information that can be found on the world wide web through the
browser. People can get equal access to information, which also establishes
the foundation for the construction of digital society.
